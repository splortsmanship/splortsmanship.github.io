Season Ten Election

  • Several announcements are released alongside the Election results
    • The Baltimore Crabs have been Called Up. Good Luck on your Journey! They shall be replaced in the ILB with the Tokyo Lift.
      • The Baltimore Crabs are removed from the game, and replaced by the Tokyo Lift, with all new players (with the exception of Wyatt Quitter, see below)
    • The Shelled One's Pods have fallen back to the plane!
      • The players from the Pods are redistributed at random to teams in the League, with no one team getting more than one new player. These players are added to their rosters without removing preexisting players.
    • The Deal is complete.
      • Players from the Hall Stars team are stated to have been "Released"
  • The Black Hole decree is enacted. This introduces two new weather types: Black Hole and Sun 2
    • Black Hole: Whenever a team scores 10 runs in a game, that team's score is brought back to zero, but the Black Hole removes a win from the opposing team
    • Sun 2: Whenever a team scores 10 runs in a game, the team's score is brought back to zero and Sun 2 grants them a bonus win
  • All teams now have the Total Fairness modifier
    • Total Fairness: Each team will win only one Blessing, and will be Happy with what they get.
      • This blessing is Seasonal, and looks like it will apply to Season Eleven election results
  • The Microphone begins to Tweet
    • Jaylen / hi / we did it / they're free / what do we do now? / wait where are the Crabs?

Season Eleven

  • Black Hole and Sun 2 begin to affect games, playing havoc with win rates: one team may be recorded as having played many more or less games than another due to the addition/removal of wins. This also allows team statistics to be calculated using a metric of wins per win.
    • The main season is relatively uneventful due to the lack of other weather
  • The Telescope appears on the Bulletin page of the main site, displaying a win-loss count. The existence of blaseball2.com is discovered, which hosts an image of the Baltimore Crabs logo behind a gate, surrounded by clouds. Clicking one of the clouds reveals The 1-Blood Blagonball. General assumption is that the Crabs are playing Blaseball against an unknown opponent in (via the Comissioner's Twitter) "the Big Leagues".
  • The Hellmouth Sunbeams claim their first championship title

Grand Siesta

  • Blaseball enters a Grand Siesta, the duration of which is unspecified. However, due to the nature of the community, the break is not as restful as one would assume, and several events occur therein:
    • The Court Case/Death of Parker MacMillan III
      • In the Blaseball Discord, judge_keeper_sins holds court for three separate trials, though the main focus is on the Mills v Macmillan III:
        • The Mills v. MacMillan III: On behalf of the 99%, the New York Millennials sue the Commissioner (and, by proxy, the Boss), for failing to redistribute wealth for the past two seasons according to the "Eat the Rich" Decree
          • VERDICT: The Coin is found guilty, but MacMillan II is innocent. The coins owed will be distributed, to a degree, to the 99% via an increase to passive income. However, despite his innocence being declared, MacMillan III had been incinerated by ump_admin_pope_sunman (whose name evolved to include the admin title during the Wiki Team's case, see below) before the trial's end. The "simulation" of the Commissioner went rogue, and MacMillan III was promptly replaced by MacMillan IIII
            • Several in-game effects are applied as a result of events within the trial: Thomas Dracaena of the New York Millennials is to be given additional baserunning, as the court wants to "see more data crimes." For her legal counsel, Beans McBlase of the Canada Moist Talkers is to be given additional baserunning as well. Lawyer Case Sports is to be reinstated to the Mexico City Wild Wings lineup, having been sent to the Shadows at the end of Season 9 due to the Disappearing Acts Blessing.
        • The Firefighters v. Dr. Mr. U. P. Q. Sunman, Ph.D, JD, esq.: The Firefighters sue ump_pope_sunman for Incinerating all three of their chats and the users therein for referring to sunman as "Ump Poop Sunman"
        • Wiki Team v. Dr. Mr. U. P. Q. Sunman, Ph.D, JD, esq.: The Wiki Team sues ump_pope_sunman for his hand in making their jobs extremely difficult - events such as the Wyatt Masoning, The Mexico City Wild Wings naming debacle, and frequent Incinerations
  • The Coffee Cup is announced, which redistributes active players into new, temporary teams based on their coffee preference. Three bonus teams flesh out the roster as well: SIBR's team, the Data Witches, and the fanart community's team, the Pandemonium Artists. The third team is left unannounced. Games will occur on Tuesdays.
