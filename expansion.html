Season Twelve

Lateseason

  • A new weather type appears: Flooding. This occasionally causes a "surge of Immateria" to wash all current baserunners off of the bases. When this happens, it also occasionally sends a player Elsewhere, giving them the Elsewhere modification. A player who is Elsewhere is removed from play for an indefinite amount of time, having a chance to return to play during another game
    • Elsewhere: This player is Elsewhere. They'll find their own way back.

Postseason

  • The Hades Tigers claim their third Championship Title

Election

  • The Boss appears
    • Congratulations Hades Tigers! / Great news! / We have everything under control. / Thanks to Our swift action / Things seem to be levelling off. / Rejoice! / You can expect less Flooding in the Future! / Meanwhile Our Food and Beverage Director is busy cooking up new tasty treats. / And what an Election! / The Fans have spoken / And We answered. / Smooth sailing ahead.
  • The Based Evolution decree passes
    • FROM NURSERY TO VAULT / PILLARS QUAKE / ACCRETION / FIRST STEPS / ROUNDING FIRST
      • Rather than Ascending (cancelled after the Shelled One's defeat), teams that win three Championships will now Evolve. A team that "rounds the evolutionary bases" will go "Home"
        • The Hades Tigers gain the Ambush modification, and all of their Players gain Base 1 Evolution
          • Ambush: When a player is incinerated in a game this team is playing, a Hall of Flame player will join their Shadows.
  • The first round of Wills passes, causing many Player swaps between teams. Two new Modifications come into play as well:
    • Players who benefit from the Trust Will receive the Flippers Modification
      • Flippers: When immateria floods, this Runner will score instead of being cleared from the Bases.
    • Pudge Nakamoto, who is targeted by the Revoke Will, receives the Roamin' Modification and moves to the Charleston Shoe Thieves
      • Roamin': This player has a chance of Roamin' to a new team at the end of each season.
  • The "The Big Sip" and "Uncle Indemnity" Blessings recruit two Coffee Cup-exclusive players from the BC Noir team to the San Francisco Lovers and New York Millennials respectively: Liquid Friend and Uncle Plasma. Both players have the Hard Boiled Modification
    • Hard Boiled: This player was boiled a little bit before hatching
