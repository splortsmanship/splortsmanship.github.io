Floodwaters continue to rise across Canada

by CREED SOMERSET

Canadians find themselves building ever upward as the Canadian floodwaters grow ever deeper with no sign of receding. The country's infrastructure has begun to shape itself around what the Public Figures are calling "the new norm", with boardwalks and sea taxis quickly taking the place of sidewalks and cars. Herald correspondents attempted to reach out to the Corporate Entities responsible for these new developments, but were unable to find the groups responsible.

Prime Minister [REDACTED] has asked citizens to stay calm, and said to enjoy their "exciting new amphibious lifestyles." This announcement has prompted many Canadians to look in the mirror and ask themselves why something feels off, if something about them has changed.
