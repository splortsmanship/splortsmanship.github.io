The Teams

Teams are organized according to the players' preferred coffee type, the exceptions being The Society Data Witches, The Pandemonium Artists, and The Real Game Band

  • Atlético Latte: "Teamwork makes the steam work"
    • Players that prefer Lattes
  • Cold Brew Crew: "Blaseball Go Brrrrrr"
    • Players that prefer Cold Brew
  • Cream & Sugar United: "There's no I in Cream... or Sugar"
    • Players that prefer Cream & Sugar
  • Society Data Witches: "Derive the Stars"
    • The Data Witches belong to SIBR (the Society for Internet Blaseball Research)
    • This team has the Affinity for Crows modifier, making players hit/pitch 50% better during Birds weather
  • Royal PoS: "Oh, Sweet"
    • Players that prefer Plenty of Sugar
  • Milk Proxy Society: "Not Milk?"
    • Players that prefer Milk Substitute
  • FWXBC: "We Forfeit!"
    • Players that prefer Flat Whites
  • Pandemonium Artists: "Paint the Town Dead!"
    • The Pandemonium Artists belong to the fanartist community. This makes them an extremely large team (the team has only 16 active players, but a large roster in the shadows), and they possess the Well Rested Modifier
      • Whenever a player on this team loses the Tired modification, they'll swap with a player in the Shadows.
  • Real Game Band: "what"
    • The Real Game Band's players are representations of members of the dev team, the Keepers on the Discord, and other key community members. The current Commissioner, Parker MacMillan IIII, is also on the team, as are several in-joke characters (Ball Clark, Cory Thirteen)

Round One

The Coffee Cup is played in a best of five bracket format, with a round being played every Tuesday

  • Every game taking place during the Coffee Cup has the Coffee weather type
    • During Coffee weather, players can get Beaned by various kinds of coffee. This makes them either Wired or Tired, which, if not replaced by the opposing Modifier, gets removed at the end of every game
      • Wired: When a Run scores, each Wired player involved in the play (the Scoring Baserunner, Current Batter, or Current Pitcher) will make the run worth 0.5 more.
      • Tired: When a Run scores, each Tired player involved in the play (the Scoring Baserunner, Current Batter, or Current Pitcher) will make the run worth 0.5 less.
        • This makes it possible for teams to score partial runs, as well as statistically introducing "runs per run"
  • Players on the Real Game Band team occasionally get "Percolated by the Tractor Bean." This removes them from play
    • Notably, this affects all of the team's Pitchers, and they are assigned Electric Kettle as their new sole pitcher
    • This also affects Parker MacMillan IIII, whose Twitter account goes dark, much as it did upon Parker MacMillan III's Incineration during The Mills v. MacMillan III
      • The Player Oops All Keepers (a representation of the Discord's moderators) receives a slightly different message:
        • Oops All Keepers was Percolated by the Tractor Bean! Oops All Keepers was fired into outer space!
